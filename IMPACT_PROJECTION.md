# Impact Projection: Multilingual Welfare Chatbot for Rural India

## Executive Summary
This document outlines the projected socio-economic impact of deploying an AI-powered welfare chatbot in a typical rural district in India. By bridging the information gap identified by NITI Aayog, the chatbot acts as a force multiplier for government service delivery.

---

## Section 1: Methodology & Reach Modeling
Our projections are grounded in the **NITI Aayog 2023** report, which highlights that while schemes are well-funded, awareness remains the primary bottleneck—with fewer than **40%** of eligible beneficiaries fully aware of their entitlements.

### **Model Parameters (Single District Case Study)**
*   **Total Population**: 500,000
*   **Rural Demographic (60%)**: 300,000 citizens
*   **Eligible Beneficiary Pool (30% of Rural)**: 90,000 people
*   **Chatbot Deployment Goal**: Target a **15% uplift** in scheme uptake through improved awareness and document readiness.
*   **Net New Beneficiaries**: 13,500 people per district.

## Section 3: Three-Phase Scale-Up Roadmap

### **Phase 1: Pilot District (Months 0–6)**
*   **Goal**: Deployment in one high-rural-density district (e.g., Gorakhpur or Coimbatore).
*   **Focus**: Testing the **70% Comprehension Target** with 5,000 initial users.
*   **Action**: Partnership with local Common Service Centres (CSCs) to place QR codes at help desks.

### **Phase 2: State NGO Partnership (Months 6–18)**
*   **Goal**: Expansion to 5–10 districts via established NGO networks.
*   **Focus**: Adding more regional dialects (Telugu, Punjabi) and offline capabilities.
*   **Action**: Training "Gram Mitras" (Village Friends) to use the bot as a toolkit for their door-to-door outreach.

### **Phase 3: Government API Integration (Months 18+)**
*   **Goal**: Integration with **MyScheme.gov.in** and **UMANG** APIs.
*   **Focus**: Real-time application status tracking and direct digital locker (DigiLocker) document fetching.
*   **Action**: Transitioning from a purely informational tool to a transactional "One-Click Apply" portal for all Indian citizens.
